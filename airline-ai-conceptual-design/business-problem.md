# Business Problem

## Industry Context

Airlines operate in highly dynamic environments where weather conditions, operational constraints, air traffic congestion, and maintenance issues frequently cause flight delays. These disruptions negatively impact customer satisfaction, operational efficiency, and revenue.

## Core Business Problems

Problem 1: Flight Delay Uncertainty

* Customers lack real-time insights into delay probability.

* Airline support teams are overwhelmed during disruptions.

* Manual delay estimation is reactive, not predictive.

Problem 2: Customer Support Overload

* High call volume during weather events.

* Long wait times reduce customer satisfaction.

* Support agents spend time answering repetitive questions.

Problem 3: Fragmented Data Systems

* Weather data from one system.

* Operational data from another.

* No unified AI-driven prediction model.

## Business Impact

* Increased operational costs due to reactive delay management
* Decreased Net Promoter Score (NPS)
* Customer churn to competing airlines
* Loss of revenue from missed connections and rebookings

## Limitations of Current Approach

* Static notifications do not predict delays
* Customer support is primarily reactive
* No intelligent conversational automation

## Opportunity for AI Integration

An AI-powered conversational assistant integrated with predictive analytics can:

* Predict delay probability using weather and operational data
* Provide real-time, automated customer responses
* Reduce support load through intelligent automation
* Improve passenger experience through proactive communication
